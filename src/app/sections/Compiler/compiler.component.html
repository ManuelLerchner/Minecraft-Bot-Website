Compiler:

As previously mentionend, building the state-machine from scratch is not a very pleasing task. so I decided to create a more abstract way of programming the minecraft bot, which allows us to use high-level abstractions like an if, while and try statement.
I had the idea, that insteady of writing the states and transitions by hand, it would be much more interseting if a "compiler" could handle that boring job. You just have to give it a high-level abstraction of the things your minecraft bot should do and the compiler assembles the underlying state-machine for you.
